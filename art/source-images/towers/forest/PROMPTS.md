# Forest tower generation prompts

All six Forest tower images have been generated with the built-in
image-generation workflow, one generation per tower. Their magenta sources are
stored beside this file; processed runtime PNGs are in
`src/assets/towers/worlds/forest/`.

Shared style and constraints:

> Cute polished 3D mobile-game tower icon; chunky readable silhouette; softly
> beveled clay-like materials; dark clean outline; gentle cream highlights;
> child-friendly and original. One tower only, centered in a three-quarter
> elevated view with the entire compact circular base visible and generous even
> padding. Perfectly flat solid `#ff00ff` chroma-key background; no gradient,
> texture, floor, lighting variation, cast/contact shadow, text, logo, watermark,
> or extra objects. Do not use magenta in the subject.

## Mycelium Network

Compact cluster of purple and cream mushrooms linked by faint glowing fungal
strands on a mossy circular base. Palette: forest green, cream, lavender, violet.

## Pollinator Post

Sturdy wooden hive post with a honeycomb launcher, tiny golden bee emblems,
flowers, and leaves on a compact circular garden base. Palette: honey gold,
cream, leafy green, natural wood.

## Canopy Guardian

Compact leafy treetop lookout with layered emerald leaves, a sturdy branch
launcher, and one large protective leaf canopy on a mossy stone base. Palette:
emerald, fern green, cream, natural brown.

## Root Snare

Ancient knotted root tower curling around a softly glowing teal seed crystal,
with several sturdy grasping roots contained within a circular soil-and-stone
base. Palette: bark brown, moss green, cream, teal glow.

## Seed Slinger

Friendly plant turret with a round seed-pod hopper and two curved leaf-powered
slings, dotted with burr seeds on a garden base. Palette: fresh green, warm tan,
coral accents, cream.

## Weathered Oak

Small ancient oak guardian with a broad worn trunk, wise knot-like face, strong
branches, sparse layered leaves, and an acorn energy core on a heavy circular
root base. Palette: deep bark brown, olive, forest green, amber, cream.

## Generic future-world placeholder

Extremely plain neutral training turret: simple light-grey cylinder, short round
barrel, single blue indicator, undecorated circular base. No world-specific
symbols, plants, letters, numbers, gears, space objects, or musical details.
