# Wizino TD collection card artwork

Generated with Codex's built-in image-generation tool on 2026-07-23. The
runtime assets are optimized WebP copies in `src/assets/cards/starters/`.
All card names, questions, answers, rarity labels, and stats are rendered in
HTML/CSS rather than baked into the artwork.

## Shared direction

Premium polished 3D-painterly fantasy character art for a children's
educational tower-defense game. Rounded, friendly proportions; strong central
silhouettes; sophisticated collectible-card finish; no card frame, text,
letters, numerals, logos, trademarks, or watermarks.

## Verdant Webkeeper

An owl-bear forest guardian in moss-and-leaf armour, holding a glowing seed
orb in a luminous clearing. Fine golden-green energy threads connect plants
and distant woodland silhouettes to suggest a food web. Emerald, moss, teal,
and warm gold palette.

Source: `verdant-webkeeper.png`

## Fulcrum Forgeback

An armadillo-tortoise engineer in rounded bronze-and-teal mechanical armour,
holding a compact lever tool in a magical workshop. A simple balanced lever
mechanism appears in the background. Copper, amber, teal, and cyan palette.

Source: `fulcrum-forgeback.png`

## Runequill Griffin

A young cobalt-and-violet griffin holding a luminous feather quill in an
enchanted library garden. Blank parchment, floating quills, and glowing sound
wave ribbons reinforce language learning without generated writing. Violet,
cobalt, cyan, and warm gold palette.

Source: `runequill-griffin.png`

## Sequence Sprite — holographic starter

A friendly translucent crystal sprite guiding repeating trails of coloured
orbs and shapes through a floating puzzle garden. Iridescent sapphire,
ultraviolet, aqua, pearl-rainbow, and restrained gold palette. The animated
holographic foil is added in CSS so it remains responsive and respects reduced
motion.

Source: `sequence-sprite.png`

## Enchanted answer-side base

A reusable symmetrical midnight-blue enchanted-vellum surface with sapphire
inlay, restrained gold filigree, corner crystals, and a calm central oval for
overlaid answer copy. No generated outer frame or text; both are rendered in
code.

Source: `enchanted-card-back.png`
